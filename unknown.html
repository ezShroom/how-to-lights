Your lights switch when flipped

This process may vary depending on the building, and it may even be different within the same building, since this country doesn't have a clear standard. Here's how to turn on the lights for either direction:

  1. Locate the light switch.
  2. Toggle the light switch to the "on" position.
  3. Enjoy the light!

To turn them back off:

  1. Locate the light switch.
  2. Toggle the light switch to the "off" position.
  3. Enjoy the darkness!

If one of these processes don't work, try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Flip the light switch the other way.
  2. Check the light bulb.
  3. Check the circuit breaker.
  4. Call an electrician.
