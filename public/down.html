Your lights flip down

This is occasionally different depending on the building, but in most places, the lights will flip down. Here's how to do it:

  1. Find the light switch.
  2. Flip the light switch down.
  3. Enjoy the light!

To turn them back off:

  1. Locate the light switch.
  2. Flip the light switch up.
  3. Enjoy the darkness!

Some light switches may be different to this, such as ones that allow you to adjust brightness. In this case, to get it right the first time, you should consult the manual that came with your light switch, or whoever installed it if you did not do this yourself.

If one of these processes don't work, try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Flip the light switch the other way.
  2. Check the light bulb.
  3. Check the circuit breaker.
  4. Call an electrician.
